Project Overview

The Trumansburg Urban Imprint project represents a comprehensive approach to village redevelopment in Tompkins County, New York. This design initiative addresses commercial development, residential needs, and community infrastructure while respecting the existing urban fabric and natural environment.

Project Context

Located along US Route 96 and Route 227 in Tompkins County, the project site encompasses diverse zoning districts including residential, commercial, and mixed-use areas. The design carefully considers the natural landscape features including wetlands and stream buffers.

With significant worker flow dynamics (718 outflow vs. 496 inflow), the project aims to create economic opportunities that better serve the local population while maintaining the village's character and environmental integrity.
